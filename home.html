Romeo & juliet

William Shakespeare

It is a tragic love story where the two main characters, Romeo and Juliet, are supposed to be sworn enemies but fall in love. Due to their families' ongoing conflict, they cannot be together, so they kill themselves because they cannot cope with being separated from one another./p>

aladdin

Antoine Galland

Aladdin requests to be freed from the cave and after his wish is granted, he goes home. By chance, Aladdin rubs the lamp, and a genie appears and asks what he desires. Aladdin asks for food, and a banquet appears before him. Soon after, Aladdin comes across the daughter of the Sultan, a princess, and falls in love.
